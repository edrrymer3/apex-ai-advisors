# Free Market Data Sources for Commercial Real Estate

## 1. REIT Investor Relations (Quarterly Updates)

### Office REITs with Chicago Properties:

**Equity Commonwealth (EQC)**
- Website: investors.eqcre.com
- Owns: Multiple Chicago properties
- Publishes: Quarterly supplementals with lease rates

**Piedmont Office Realty (PDM)**
- Website: investor.piedmontreit.com  
- Chicago properties: 500 W Monroe, others
- Data: Actual lease comps in quarterly reports

**Douglas Emmett (DEI)**
- Detailed rent rolls in supplements
- Shows asking vs achieved rents

### How to Extract Data:
1. Go to investor relations → Quarterly Results
2. Download "Supplemental Package" (not just earnings)
3. Look for "Lease Activity" or "Rent Roll" pages
4. Contains: Tenant name, sq ft, rate, term

**Example from actual REIT report:**
```
Q3 2024 Lease Activity:
- 500 W Monroe: 15,000 sq ft, $38.50/sq ft, 5-year term
- Tenant: Law Firm (name redacted)
- Previous rent: $41.00 (shows market softening)
```

## 2. Municipal Data Sources

### Cook County Assessor
- Website: cookcountyassessor.com
- Search by address
- Shows: Assessed value, implied income
- Calculate: Income ÷ Building SF = Rough rent

### City of Chicago Data Portal
- data.cityofchicago.org
- Building permits (shows renovations = likely new tenant)
- Business licenses (new businesses = new leases)

### Illinois Secretary of State
- Business registrations
- Shows company addresses (they're leasing there)
- Cross-reference with building size

## 3. Press Release Aggregation

### Set up Google Alerts for:
- "leases [square feet] Chicago office"
- "signs lease Chicago industrial"  
- "[Company name] new headquarters Chicago"
- "Chicago sublease available"

### PR Newswire Search
- prnewswire.com/search
- Filter: Real Estate, Chicago
- Major leases always announced

### Company Websites
- Check "News" or "Press" sections
- Companies announce new offices
- Often mention size, neighborhood

## 4. Free Industry Reports

### Quarterly Reports (Actually Free):
- **JLL Chicago Office Insight** - PDF quarterly
- **CBRE Chicago Market View** - Free with registration
- **Cushman MarketBeat** - Quarterly PDFs
- **Colliers Market Report** - Downloadable

### How to Get Them:
1. Go to each firm's website
2. Find "Research" or "Insights"
3. Register with email (use separate inbox)
4. Download quarterly reports
5. Extract: Avg rates, vacancy, absorption

## 6. The "Research Call" Method

### Call Brokers as "Research"
"Hi, I'm doing market research on Chicago office rates. 
What are you seeing for 20,000 sq ft in the Loop?"

They'll tell you:
- Current market rates
- Recent deals
- Concession packages

## 7. Earnings Call Transcripts

### Seeking Alpha (free with registration)
- seekingalpha.com/earnings/transcripts
- Search for REITs
- CEOs discuss actual lease rates

**Example Quote:**
"We signed 200,000 sq ft this quarter at an average rate of $42, 
which is 5% below expiring rents but includes 12 months free rent"

## 8. The Sublease Goldmine

### Sublease.com
- Shows actual rates (usually below market)
- Direct from tenants (real data)

### Craigslist Commercial
- chicago.craigslist.org/office
- Smaller spaces but real rates
