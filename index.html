COMMON QUESTIONS

Straight Answers

Does it cost me anything to work with you? +
No. Tenant rep brokers are compensated by the landlord through the commission structure built into every commercial lease. You pay nothing directly — and our incentive is to get you the best deal possible.
What markets do you cover? +
Primarily the Twin Cities metro — Minneapolis CBD, suburbs (Eden Prairie, Plymouth, Minnetonka, Edina, Bloomington), and the full industrial corridor. We can also assist with multi-market searches through our national network.
We just want to renew our current lease. Can you still help? +
Absolutely — and this is where tenants leave the most money on the table. Without a competitive search running in parallel, your landlord has zero pressure to offer market concessions. We run the market, use competing options as leverage, and most renewal clients save 15–25% versus going direct.
How long does a search typically take? +
For a straightforward office search under 15,000 SF: 6–10 weeks from kickoff to signed lease. Larger searches run 10–16 weeks. Industrial with specific requirements (loading docks, power, clear heights) may take longer. We set realistic expectations upfront.
What does "AI-powered" actually mean for my search? +
Our AI tools handle the work that used to take days: instant market surveys, proposal comparison with net effective rent math, custom negotiation playbooks, and market reports ready to share. You get faster decisions, better data, and a broker who isn’t buried in spreadsheets.
