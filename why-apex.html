Common Questions

Straight Answers

Does it cost me anything to work with you? +
No. Like all tenant rep brokers, we're paid by the landlord out of the commission structure built into every lease. You pay nothing directly — and our incentive is to get you the best deal, since happy tenants refer other tenants.
What markets do you cover? +
We're based in the Twin Cities and primarily serve the Minneapolis-St. Paul metro — downtown, suburban, and industrial submarkets. We can also assist with multi-market searches through our network.
We already have a broker. Can you help us evaluate their work? +
Absolutely. Use our free Proposal Comparison tool to run the numbers on any deal you've been presented — you'll see the net effective rent, total cost, and whether the concessions are in line with the market. No commitment required.
How is AI involved in the process? +
Our AI tools handle the analytical work that used to take days: market surveys, proposal comparisons, negotiation playbooks, RFP generation, and market reports. Your advisor focuses on relationships and negotiations. The result is faster decisions with better data.
What if we just want to renew our current lease? +
Renewals are where tenants leave the most money on the table. Without a competitive search, your landlord has zero pressure to offer market concessions. We run a full market survey and use competing options as leverage — even if you want to stay. Most clients save 15–25% on renewals versus going direct.
How long does a typical search take? +
For a straightforward office search under 15,000 SF in the Twin Cities: 6–10 weeks from kickoff to signed lease. Larger or more complex searches run 10–16 weeks. Industrial with specific infrastructure requirements may take longer. We'll give you a realistic timeline at our first meeting.
