💡 Pro Tips

🎯 Include a deadline — asking for proposals by a specific date creates urgency and better response rates.
💰 Don't show your max budget — let the market respond first, then use budget as a filter.
📊 Request comparison format — ask landlords to respond in a standardized format so you can compare apples to apples.
🏢 Send to 6–10 properties — creates competition among landlords and strengthens your negotiating position.
⏱️ Time it right — Q1 and Q3 tend to get better landlord engagement than year-end.
